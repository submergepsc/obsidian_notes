To paste a clip on single click rather than double click change the following settings
1. Go to Options - Quick Paste Keyboard
2. Find "Paste Selected" in the list and select
3. In the combo select "Click"
4. In the bottom combo change "Double Click" to "Click"
5. Click Assign
