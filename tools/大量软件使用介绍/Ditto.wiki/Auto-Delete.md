**Two ways to auto delete clips, Options - General**
1. Maximum number of saved copies
2. Paste entries expire after X days
**Clips will not auto delete when any of the following are set**
1. Clip properties - Never auto delete
2. Clip properties - Hot Key
3. In a group
4. Marked as a sticky clip
#### Auto delete will occur in the background, not immediately, clips will be deleted after your computer has been idle for some time.
